Chess Coaching!

Welcome to our Chess Coaching program!

We believe that chess is more than just a game but also a tool for personal growth, strategic thinking, and community building.

Our coaching services aim to provide quality chess instruction to players of all levels, whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced player looking to enhance your skills.

Our Mission


At Chess for Kids our mission is to make chess accessible to everyone, regardless of age, background, or socioeconomic status. Through our coaching program, we aim to:



Introduce chess to beginners and provide them with a solid foundation in the game.



Help intermediate players refine their skills and strategies to advance their gameplay.



Support advanced players in mastering advanced tactics and achieving their competitive goals.



Foster a supportive and inclusive chess community where players can learn, grow, and connect with others who share their passion for the game.



Our Coaching Approach


Our coaching approach is tailored to meet the needs and goals of each player. Whether you're looking to improve your opening repertoire, sharpen your tactical skills, or develop a better understanding of positional play, our experienced coaches are here to help you reach your full potential.

What We Offer


One-on-One Coaching: Personalized coaching sessions tailored to your skill level and goals.



Group Classes: Interactive group classes covering various aspects of chess strategy and gameplay.



Tactics Training: Practice sessions focused on improving your tactical awareness and calculation skills.



Game Analysis: In-depth analysis of your games to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.



Tournament Preparation: Guidance and support to help you prepare for upcoming chess tournaments and competitions.



Chess Resources: Access to a wealth of educational materials, including articles, videos, and recommended reading.
